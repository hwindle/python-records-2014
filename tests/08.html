Table View Right Click menu

  1. Open Records, open a previous database.
  2. Go to the table view.
  3. Right click anywhere in a row.
  4. The cut, copy paste items are disabled when no data is selected. Verify that they are grey.
  5. Try deleting a row by right clicking any row and selecting 'Delete row'. An 'are you sure?' dialog should pop up. Click 'yes'.
